# SEO Indexing Guide for TaxOFF Partners

## Why Your Site Isn't Showing Up Yet

Your SEO is properly implemented, but **search engines need to discover and index your site first**. This can take:
- **Google**: 1-7 days (sometimes longer)
- **Yandex**: 1-14 days
- **Other search engines**: Varies

## Steps to Get Indexed Faster

### 1. Submit to Google Search Console

1. Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. Add your property: `https://taxoff.vercel.app`
3. Verify ownership (choose HTML tag method)
4. Submit your sitemap: `https://taxoff.vercel.app/sitemap.xml`
5. Request indexing for your homepage

### 2. Submit to Yandex Webmaster

1. Go to [Yandex Webmaster](https://webmaster.yandex.com/)
2. Add your site: `https://taxoff.vercel.app`
3. Verify ownership
4. Submit sitemap: `https://taxoff.vercel.app/sitemap.xml`

### 3. Submit to Bing Webmaster Tools

1. Go to [Bing Webmaster Tools](https://www.bing.com/webmasters)
2. Add your site
3. Submit sitemap

### 4. Create Backlinks

- Share on social media (Facebook, LinkedIn, Telegram)
- Submit to business directories
- Get listed on local business sites
- Share in relevant forums/communities

### 5. Check Your Sitemap

Visit these URLs to verify they work:
- `https://taxoff.vercel.app/sitemap.xml`
- `https://taxoff.vercel.app/robots.txt`

2. **Check robots.txt**: Visit `https://taxoff.vercel.app/robots.txt`
3. **Test in Google**: Search `site:taxoff.vercel.app` to see if indexed

## When You Get Your Custom Domain (taxoff.uz)

1. Add domain in Vercel
2. Set environment variable: `NEXT_PUBLIC_BASE_URL=https://taxoff.uz`
3. Update Google Search Console with new domain
4. Submit new sitemap: `https://taxoff.uz/sitemap.xml`

## Expected Timeline

- **Initial indexing**: 1-7 days
- **Full indexing**: 2-4 weeks

## Tips to Speed Up Indexing

1. **Regular content updates**: Post new blog posts/news regularly
2. **Internal linking**: Link between pages on your site
3. **Social signals**: Share content on social media
4. **Local SEO**: Add location-specific content (Toshkent, Uzbekistan)
